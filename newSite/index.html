Build a startup from the ground up.

Sept. 23-25, University of Illinois

54.io is a 3-day event where students from different backgrounds can come together to form teams and launch a successful startup. No experience required. Just bring yourself, some friends and good ideas. Refreshments will be provided.

WHO SHOULD APPLY?

Whether you’re an artist or a social worker, a doctor or an entrepreneur, want to change the world with your ideas, or simply just want to have some fun, 54 is for you!

Have an idea? 54 is the perfect place to meet team members that can help bring your idea to life.
Don’t have an idea? No problem. Bring a unique perspective. Teams will need designers, developers, marketers, and you!

WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

54 builds strong friendships and sustainable businesses that last even after the event is over. Startups are powered aircrafts, not projectiles.

Cash Prizes: The top team will walk away with some money to help turn their idea into a reality.
Dinner: The top three teams get a dinner with one of our sponsors.
Real Startups: Some of our past winners include Amber Waves, Mymetrics, and FreeSkies. Who kows what could happen?
